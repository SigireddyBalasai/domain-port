## Introduction: Why Audio Matters in Security Cameras

A picture is worth a thousand words – but sometimes you need the words too. Two-way audio in security cameras lets you speak through the camera: instruct the delivery driver, call the kids, warn an intruder, or just talk to your pet.

Audio in security cameras has two main functions: **Communication** (speaking with people in front of the camera) and **Deterrence** (sirens or voice warnings). We tested the best wireless cameras with audio features.
