Night vision failures are the #1 "camera works day, useless at night" complaint. This guide walks through every failure mode with diagnostics, fixes, and when to replace.

<Badge variant="outline">Quick Triage</Badge>
1. **IR LEDs on?** (red glow at night) → No = Power/IR board / Yes =
Optics/Config 2. **Image white/gray?** → IR reflection (angle/distance) 3.
**Image black/noisy?** → IR too weak / shutter too slow / sensor failing 4.
**Color night vision off?** → Ambient light too low / setting disabled

## 1. IR LEDs Not Illuminating (No Red Glow)

### 1.1 Verify IR Status

<Accordion type="single" collapsible>
  <AccordionItem value="check-ir">
    <AccordionTrigger>How to Check IR LEDs</AccordionTrigger>
    <AccordionContent>

      **Visual:** Stand in front of camera at night → look for **faint red
      glow** (850 nm) or **no visible glow** (940 nm "covert"). **Phone Camera
      Test:** Point phone camera at camera → IR appears **bright purple/white**
      on screen (phone sensors see near-IR). **App Status:** Camera web UI / NVR
      → "IR Status" / "Night Mode" → should show "On" or "Auto".

    </AccordionContent>

  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

### 1.2 Causes & Fixes

| Cause                              | Diagnosis                                     | Fix                                                                         |
| ---------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **IR Cut Filter Stuck (Day Mode)** | Image colored at night, no IR glow            | Power cycle (12V/PoE off 30s). Tap camera gently. Firmware update.          |
| **Photoresistor/LDR Failed**       | IR never auto-enables; manual "On" works      | Cover LDR (small clear window) → if IR triggers, replace LDR ($2) or camera |
| **IR LED Driver Board Dead**       | 12V/PoE present, LEDs cold, manual "On" fails | Replace camera (board not serviceable)                                      |
| **Power Insufficient for IR**      | Day OK, night reboots / IR flickers           | Check voltage at camera under IR load (see §1.3)                            |
| **Firmware Bug**                   | Recent update broke auto-IR                   | Rollback firmware; vendor support ticket                                    |
| **Covert IR (940 nm)**             | No red glow, phone test shows purple          | Normal — 940 nm is invisible to eye                                         |

### 1.3 Voltage Drop Under IR Load (Critical)

**IR LEDs draw 2–5W extra.** Camera runs day, crashes at night.

<Callout type="warning">

**Test:** Multimeter at camera power input (PoE: use PoE tester). Day voltage vs Night (IR on) voltage. **Drop >0.5V (12V) or >2V (PoE 48V) = cable too long / gauge too small / switch budget exceeded.**

</Callout>

| Architecture               | Max IR Load               | Fix                                                     |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **PoE (802.3af 15.4W)**    | ~4W IR                    | Upgrade to PoE+ (30W) injector/switch                   |
| **12V DC (18 AWG, 50 ft)** | 2A IR                     | Shorten run / 14 AWG / local 12V supply                 |
| **Battery Cam**            | IR kills battery in hours | Disable IR; use ambient light / external IR illuminator |

## 2. IR Whiteout / Washed Out / Gray Image

**Symptom:** Night image = bright white/gray blob, no detail. IR reflects off nearby surface.

<Accordion type="single" collapsible>
  <AccordionItem value="whiteout-causes">
    <AccordionTrigger>Common Reflection Sources</AccordionTrigger>
    <AccordionContent>

      | Surface | Distance | Fix | |---------|----------|-----| | **Wall /
      Siding / Fascia** | 6–24" | Angle camera **down 15–30°**; move out 12–18"
      (extension arm) | | **Eave / Soffit** | 0–12" | Mount **below** soffit,
      not flush to it | | **Window / Glass** | Any | **Never** aim through glass
      (IR reflects 100%) | | **Spider Web / Dust on Dome** | On lens | Clean
      monthly; fleece cover over IR ring | | **Rain / Snow / Fog** | Airborne |
      External IR illuminator (mounted away from lens) | | **White Trim /
      Gutter** | 12–36" | Paint flat black (BBQ paint) or mask with tape | |
      **License Plate (Retroreflective)** | 20–50 ft | Angle down; external IR;
      LPR camera with polarizer |

    </AccordionContent>

  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

### 2.1 Step-by-Step Whiteout Fix

1. **Identify reflector:** Go out at night, watch IR glow on surfaces. Use phone camera to see IR spill.
2. **Re-angle:** Tilt **down 15–30°** from horizontal. Use level + protractor.
3. **Add distance:** Extension bracket (12–24") moves IR cone past near objects.
4. **External IR Illuminator:** Mount 10–20 ft away, aimed at target zone. Camera IR **OFF**. Best for license plates, gates.
5. **Smart IR (if supported):** Camera auto-reduces IR intensity for close objects. Enable in settings.
6. **IR Cut Filter Check:** Stuck "out" (night mode) = color day image. Stuck "in" (day mode) = no IR. Power cycle.

## 3. Black / Noisy / Grainy Night Image

**Symptom:** Dark, speckled, "snowy" video. No detail.

| Cause                              | Diagnosis                     | Fix                                                      |
| ---------------------------------- | ----------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| **IR Too Weak for Distance**       | Target >50% of rated IR range | External IR illuminator; move camera closer              |
| **Shutter Too Slow (Motion Blur)** | Ghost trails, bright streaks  | Set **Min Shutter: 1/100s** (1/250s for plates)          |
| **Gain/ISO Maxed Out**             | Noise = "snow"                | Add light (external IR, streetlight); lower max gain     |
| **Wide Dynamic Range (WDR) Off**   | Bright lights blind camera    | Enable **WDR: 120 dB+** (True WDR, not DWDR)             |
| **Dome Dirty / Scratched**         | Halo around lights            | Replace dome ($15); clean with microfiber + lens cleaner |
| **Sensor Degrading**               | Progressive over years        | Replace camera (CMOS ages, hot pixels increase)          |
| **Compression Too High**           | Blocky artifacts at night     | Bitrate: CBR 4–6 Mbps (4K) / 2–3 Mbps (2K)               |

### 3.1 Shutter Speed Cheat Sheet

| Target            | Min Shutter         | Notes                       |
| ----------------- | ------------------- | --------------------------- |
| General area      | 1/50s (1/60s US)    | Balance light vs blur       |
| Walking person    | 1/100s              | Sharp limbs                 |
| **License Plate** | **1/250s – 1/500s** | **Critical** — set manually |
| Fast vehicle      | 1/500s – 1/1000s    | Need external IR            |

<Callout type="note">

**How to set:** Camera Web UI → Image → Exposure → Shutter Speed → Manual →
Enter value. Disable "Auto" shutter.

</Callout>

## 4. Color Night Vision Failures

**Color Night Vision (CNV)** = Large sensor (1/1.8", 1/1.2") + low-light ISP + **ambient light required**.

| Failure Mode                    | Cause                   | Fix                                                                                                           |
| ------------------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Stays B&W (IR on)**           | Ambient &lt;1 lux       | CNV needs ~3–5 lux (streetlight, porch light). Add **warm white (3000K) LED** 10–20 ft away.                  |
| **Color noisy/grainy**          | Gain too high           | Lower max gain; add light; accept B&W IR                                                                      |
| **Color shifts (purple/green)** | White balance confused  | Set WB to "Outdoor" / "Streetlight" / Custom 3000K                                                            |
| **IR + CNV conflict**           | Both on = weird colors  | Disable IR when CNV active (auto in good cams)                                                                |
| **Not supported**               | Camera lacks CNV sensor | Check specs: needs 1/1.8"+ sensor (Reolink ColorX, Hikvision ColorVu, Dahua Full-Color, Axis Lightfinder 2.0) |

## 5. License Plate Capture (LPC) — Specialized Night Fixes

**LPC fails at night 90% due to:** IR reflection off plate + slow shutter + weak IR.

### 5.1 LPC Night Checklist

- [ ] **Camera:** 4K/8MP + optical zoom (varifocal 2.8–12mm or 5–50mm) OR dedicated LPR (2MP, 100+ fps, global shutter)
- [ ] **Shutter:** **1/250s minimum** (manual)
- [ ] **IR:** External illuminator (850 nm, 30–50° beam) **off-camera**, aimed at plate zone
- [ ] **Angle:** ≤30° off perpendicular; height 3–4 ft (plate level)
- [ ] **WDR:** 120 dB+ True WDR (handles headlights)
- [ ] **IR Cut:** Verify switching (day color / night B&W)
- [ ] **Zone:** Motion zone = plate path only (reduce false triggers)
- [ ] **Record:** CBR 6–8 Mbps (4K) / 4 Mbps (2MP LPR)

## 6. Doorbell Camera Night Issues

| Problem                    | Cause                          | Fix                                                         |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Visitor face whiteout**  | IR reflects off porch/overhang | Angle wedge kit (15–25° down); disable IR → use porch light |
| **No night vision**        | Battery saving mode            | Hardwire (16–24V AC) → enables full-time IR                 |
| **Color night vision off** | Ambient too low                | Add **3000K LED** in porch fixture (not cool white)         |
| **Motion misses at night** | PIR range < IR range           | PIR ~25 ft, IR ~30 ft → reduce IR sensitivity / narrow zone |

## 7. Battery / Solar Camera Night Specifics

| Issue                          | Cause                          | Fix                                                          |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **IR kills battery in 1 week** | IR = 2–4W continuous           | Disable IR; rely on ambient + motion spotlight (Eufy, Ring)  |
| **No night vision in winter**  | Li-ion &lt;32°F stops charging | LiFePO₄ upgrade (Reolink Go PT Plus); insulated box + heater |
| **Motion only works day**      | PIR blocked by window/glass    | Mount **outside** glass; PIR needs temp differential         |
| **Spotlight doesn't trigger**  | Motion zone wrong              | Redraw zone to cover approach path; test walk                |

**Procedure:** Mount at target distance → record → review plate/face clarity.

### 8.2 IR Power Measurement

| Tool                 | Measurement                 | Pass Criteria                      |
| -------------------- | --------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **PoE Tester**       | Voltage at camera (night)   | >48V (PoE+), >52V (PoE++)          |
| **Multimeter (12V)** | Voltage at camera (night)   | >11.5V (12V nominal)               |
| **Clamp Meter**      | Current on 12V line (night) | &lt;2A (per camera)                |
| **Thermal Camera**   | IR LED temp                 | 40–60°C (working), &lt;30°C (dead) |

### 8.3 Firmware & Config Audit

```
☐ Firmware: Latest stable (not beta)
☐ IR Mode: Auto (not "On" 24/7)
☐ Smart IR: Enabled
☐ Shutter: Manual 1/100s min (1/250s for plates)
☐ WDR: True WDR On (120 dB+)
☐ Gain Max: Capped (not "Auto Max")
☐ Bitrate: CBR 4–6 Mbps (4K)
☐ IR Cut: Switching verified (day/night)
☐ Timezone/NTP: Correct (scheduled IR)
```

## 9. When to Replace vs Repair

| Symptom                     | Repair Possible?          | Replace When                                |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| No IR glow, power OK        | IR board ($50) / LDR ($2) | Camera >3 yrs, water ingress                |
| Stuck IR cut filter         | Tap/power cycle (temp)    | Mechanism worn; >2 yrs                      |
| Whiteout unfixable by angle | N/A                       | Wrong camera for location (use external IR) |
| Noisy sensor (hot pixels)   | Pixel mapping (some cams) | >5% dead pixels; >4 yrs                     |
| Color night vision failed   | Sensor/ISP issue          | Sensor too small (&lt;1/2.8"); upgrade      |
| Intermittent night reboot   | Voltage drop              | Fix cable/power; if persistent → replace    |
