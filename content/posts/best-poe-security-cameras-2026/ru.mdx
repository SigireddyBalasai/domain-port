## Введение: Why PoE?

Power over Ethernet (PoE) is the gold standard of security cameras. A single cable transmits both power and data – enabling 24/7 continuous recording without Wi-Fi issues or battery concerns.

PoE cameras are the first choice for anyone seeking absolute reliability. They are more labor-intensive to install (cable laying), but once set up, they run for years without intervention.

## PoE Infrastructure: What You Need

**PoE Switch:** All cameras connected to a central switch with PoE (802.3af/at). Ensure sufficient budget per port (at least 8W for 1080p, 12W for 4K).

**Cable:** Cat6 or better for 4K cameras. Outdoor-rated cable (CMX or PE) for exterior installations.

**NVR (optional):** Network Video Recorder for centralized recording and management of multiple cameras.
