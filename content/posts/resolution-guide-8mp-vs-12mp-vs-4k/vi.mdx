Nhieu megapixel hon khong co nghia la canh quay an ninh tot hon.

## So Sanh Do Phan Giai

| Thong so           | 4K (8MP)  | 5MP       | 12MP      |
| ------------------ | --------- | --------- | --------- |
| **Pixel**          | 3840x2160 | 2560x1920 | 4000x3000 |
| **Khoang cach ID** | 15-20m    | 9-15m     | 20-30m    |
| **Luu tru/ngay**   | 30-50 GB  | 25-35 GB  | 50-80 GB  |
| **Nhin dem**       | Kha       | Tot hon   | Kem hon   |

## Khi Nao Dung Loai Naoo

- **4K (8MP):** Tot nhat cho da so nha va doanh nghiep
- **5MP (4:3):** Tot hon cho phu doc (hanh lang)
- **12MP:** Cho khu vuc rong can phong to
- **1080p:** Du cho khong gian trong nha

## Ket Luan

4K la lua chon pho bien nhat vi can bang chi tiet va chi phi luu tru. Chi dung 12MP khi ban can chi tiet toi da.
