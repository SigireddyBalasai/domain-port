## What do we like about it?

- **360° PTZ with Motion Tracking** — The camera automatically tracks moving objects, keeping them in frame without manual intervention. At this price point, this is a rare feature.
- **Color Night Vision** — Unlike infrared-only cameras, the Mini Fox delivers full-color footage in low light, making it easier to identify people and objects.
- **Multi-User Viewing** — Share the feed with family members so everyone can monitor from their own phone.
- **Two-Way Audio** — Talk to pets, kids, or visitors through the camera's built-in speaker and microphone.
- **Price** — At ₹1,149 (77% off MRP of ₹4,999), it's one of the most affordable PTZ cameras with color night vision in India.

## What could be improved?

- **Indoor Only** — No weatherproofing rating means this camera stays inside.
- **WiFi Only** — No PoE or Ethernet option. You need a stable 2.4GHz WiFi network.
- **2.4GHz Only** — Does not support 5GHz WiFi, which may cause congestion in apartment buildings.
- **6-Month Warranty** — Shorter than the typical 1–2 year warranty on competing brands.
- **3.5/5 Rating** — User reviews are mixed, with some reporting connectivity issues and app lag.

## How do you set up the Maizic Mini Fox PTZ?

1. **Unbox and mount** — Place on a shelf or wall-mount using the included bracket.
2. **Download the Maizic app** — Available on iOS and Android.
3. **Connect to WiFi** — Follow the in-app setup to pair over 2.4GHz.
4. **Configure PTZ and motion tracking** — Set patrol points and adjust motion sensitivity.
5. **Enable storage** — Insert a microSD card or subscribe to cloud storage.
