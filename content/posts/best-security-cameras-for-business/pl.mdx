## Business Features

**ONVIF Compliance:** Guarantees compatibility between different manufacturers. Look for ONVIF Profile S (streaming) and G (recording).

**PoE:** For commercial installations, PoE is mandatory. No Wi-Fi cameras for critical areas.

**NVR Compatibility:** Ensure the cameras are compatible with your NVR.

**Vandal Protection:** IK10 rating for publicly accessible cameras.

**Remote Access:** Manage multiple locations from a central hub.

## Nasz werdykt

For most businesses, we recommend **Hikvision AcuSense** or **Dahua** cameras – professional quality with AI detection. For smaller budgets, the **Reolink RLC-822A** or **RLC-520A** are good choices. Look for ONVIF compatibility for flexibility in system selection.
