## Driveway Camera Tips

**Positioning:** Position the camera to capture the driveway from the side – not from above. Side views capture license plates better.

**Height:** 2.5-3 meters (8-10 feet) above the ground. High enough for security, low enough for license plate recognition.

**Lighting:** Good lighting (or spotlight) significantly improves license plate recognition at night.

**Rozdzielczość:** 2K is the minimum for license plates at 5-8 meters (15-25 feet). 4K for longer distances.
