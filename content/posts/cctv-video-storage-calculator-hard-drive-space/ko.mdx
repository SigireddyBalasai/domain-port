Storage is the #1 reason camera systems fail. Undersized drives = gaps in footage when you need it most. This guide gives you the exact formula, a mental calculator, and drive recommendations so you buy once and record for years.

<Badge variant="outline">Quick Formula</Badge> **Daily GB = Cameras × Mbps ×
108** (for H.265). Example: 8 cams × 4 Mbps × 108 = **3,456 GB/day** = **3.4
TB/day**.

---

## The Storage Formula (Memorize This)

### Core Equation

```
GB per Day = (Cameras × Bitrate_Mbps × 3600 × 24) / (8 × 1024)
           = Cameras × Bitrate_Mbps × 10.547
```

**Simplified (H.265):** `Cameras × Mbps × 108 = GB/day` (within 3%)

**Simplified (H.264):** `Cameras × Mbps × 216 = GB/day` (H.264 ≈ 2× H.265)

### Bitrate Reference Table (2026 Typical)

| Resolution      | Codec | FPS | Scene Complexity  | Bitrate (Mbps) |
| --------------- | ----- | --- | ----------------- | -------------- |
| **4K (8MP)**    | H.265 | 15  | Low (static)      | 3–4            |
| 4K (8MP)        | H.265 | 15  | Medium            | 4–6            |
| 4K (8MP)        | H.265 | 15  | High (trees/rain) | 6–8            |
| 4K (8MP)        | H.265 | 30  | Medium            | 8–12           |
| **2K (4MP)**    | H.265 | 15  | Medium            | 2–3            |
| 2K (4MP)        | H.265 | 30  | Medium            | 4–6            |
| **1080p (2MP)** | H.265 | 15  | Medium            | 1–2            |
| 1080p (2MP)     | H.265 | 30  | Medium            | 2–4            |
| **4K (8MP)**    | H.264 | 15  | Medium            | 8–12           |
| **1080p (2MP)** | H.264 | 30  | Medium            | 4–8            |

<Callout type="tip">

**Pro Tip:** Enable **H.265 (HEVC)** on every camera/NVR. It halves storage vs
H.264. All 2020+ cameras support it. If your NVR doesn't, upgrade the NVR.

</Callout>

## What Changes the Numbers (Multipliers)

<Accordion type="single" collapsible>
  <AccordionItem value="motion">
    <AccordionTrigger>Motion-Only Recording (Saves 50–80%)</AccordionTrigger>
    <AccordionContent>

**How it works:** Record continuous on sub-stream (low-res), full-res only on motion.

| Scenario                        | Storage vs Continuous        |
| ------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Quiet residential (5 events/hr) | **15–25%** of continuous     |
| Busy retail (50 events/hr)      | **40–60%**                   |
| Highway / parking lot           | **70–90%** (near continuous) |

**Formula:** `Continuous_GB × Motion_Factor = Actual_GB`

- Conservative: **× 0.3** (quiet)
- Typical: **× 0.5** (moderate)
- Busy: **× 0.75** (high traffic)

**Caveat:** Motion misses = no footage. Use **continuous + motion tags** (NVR records 24/7, flags motion events).

</AccordionContent>

  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem value="substream">
    <AccordionTrigger>Sub-Streams (Live View Without Storage Hit)</AccordionTrigger>
    <AccordionContent>

**Sub-stream** = Low-res (D1/720p) stream for live view / mobile. **Does not record** (or records at tiny bitrate).

| Stream    | Resolution  | Bitrate      | Purpose                          |
| --------- | ----------- | ------------ | -------------------------------- |
| **Main**  | 4K/2K/1080p | 2–8 Mbps     | **Recording** (evidence)         |
| **Sub**   | 720p/D1/CIF | 0.5–1.5 Mbps | Live view, mobile, motion detect |
| **Third** | 480p/360p   | 0.2–0.5 Mbps | Mobile backup, 3G/4G             |

**Storage impact:** Sub-stream recording adds **5–15%** if enabled. Usually **disable sub-stream recording** — use main stream only.

</AccordionContent>

  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem value="raid">
    <AccordionTrigger>RAID Overhead (Usable vs Raw)</AccordionTrigger>
    <AccordionContent>

| RAID                | Drives Min | Usable %                                | Survives   | Rebuild Risk                  |
| ------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------- |
| **RAID 1** (Mirror) | 2          | **50%**                                 | 1 drive    | Low                           |
| **RAID 5**          | 3          | **(N-1)/N** (67% @ 3, 75% @ 4)          | 1 drive    | Medium (URE on >8TB)          |
| **RAID 6**          | 4          | **(N-2)/N** (50% @ 4, 60% @ 5, 67% @ 6) | 2 drives   | **Low** (recommended ≥6 bays) |
| **RAID 10**         | 4 (even)   | **50%**                                 | 1–2 drives | Very Low                      |
| **SHR** (Synology)  | 2+         | Flexible                                | 1–2        | Low                           |

**Rule:** For 4K/8+ cams / >30 days → **RAID 6 minimum**. For 1080p/2K &lt;8 cams → RAID 1/5 OK.

**Calculation:** `Required_Raw_TB = Required_Usable_TB / RAID_Usable_%`
Example: 20 TB usable, RAID 6 (60%) → **34 TB raw** = 6× 8TB or 4× 10TB

</AccordionContent>

  </AccordionItem>
  <AccordionItem value="retention">
    
    <AccordionTrigger>Retention Tiers (Smart Storage)</AccordionTrigger>
    <AccordionContent>

Not all footage needs 90 days.

| Tier        | Retention    | What                                 | Typical % of Total  |
| ----------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------ | ------------------- |
| **Hot**     | 0–7 days     | All cameras, full res, continuous    | 30–50%              |
| **Warm**    | 8–30 days    | Motion events only, full res         | 30–40%              |
| **Cold**    | 31–365+ days | Critical cameras only, or sub-stream | 10–20%              |
| **Archive** | 1–7 years    | Incidents, exports, legal hold       | Offsite (Wasabi/B2) |

**NVR Support:** UniFi Protect, Synology Surveillance Station, Dahua DSS, Hikvision iVMS support tiered retention natively.

</AccordionContent>

  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>

## Complete Sizing Worksheet

### Step 1: List Your Cameras

| Cam       | Location   | Res   | Codec | FPS | Est. Mbps   |
| --------- | ---------- | ----- | ----- | --- | ----------- |
| 1         | Front Door | 4K    | H.265 | 15  | 4           |
| 2         | Driveway   | 4K    | H.265 | 15  | 4           |
| 3         | Backyard   | 2K    | H.265 | 15  | 3           |
| 4         | Side Gate  | 2K    | H.265 | 15  | 3           |
| 5         | Garage     | 1080p | H.265 | 30  | 2           |
| **Total** |            |       |       |     | **16 Mbps** |

### Step 2: Calculate Daily GB

`Total_Mbps × 108 = GB/day` (H.265)
`16 × 108 = 1,728 GB/day ≈ 1.7 TB/day`

### Step 3: Apply Retention & Motion Factor

| Retention                | Motion Factor | Calculation     | Required    |
| ------------------------ | ------------- | --------------- | ----------- |
| **7 days continuous**    | 1.0           | 1.7 × 7         | **11.9 TB** |
| **14 days continuous**   | 1.0           | 1.7 × 14        | **23.8 TB** |
| **30 days (motion 50%)** | 0.5           | 1.7 × 30 × 0.5  | **25.5 TB** |
| **90 days (tiered)**     | 0.35 avg      | 1.7 × 90 × 0.35 | **53.5 TB** |

### Step 4: Add RAID Overhead

| RAID               | Usable % | Raw TB Needed (for 25.5 TB usable)   |
| ------------------ | -------- | ------------------------------------ |
| RAID 1 (2 bay)     | 50%      | **51 TB** → 6× 10TB                  |
| RAID 5 (4 bay)     | 75%      | **34 TB** → 4× 10TB                  |
| **RAID 6 (6 bay)** | **60%**  | **42.5 TB** → **5× 10TB or 4× 12TB** |
| RAID 10 (4 bay)    | 50%      | 51 TB → 6× 10TB                      |

### Step 5: Pick Drives

**For this example (25.5 TB usable, RAID 6):**

- **Option A:** 5× 10 TB SkyHawk = 50 TB raw → 30 TB usable ✓ ($900)
- **Option B:** 4× 12 TB SkyHawk = 48 TB raw → 28.8 TB usable ✓ ($1,000)
- **Option C:** 6× 8 TB SkyHawk = 48 TB raw → 28.8 TB usable ✓ ($960)

## Bandwidth vs Storage (Don't Forget Upload)

If you back up to cloud (Wasabi/B2/R2):

| Cameras | Res | Main Stream Mbps | 30-Day Cloud TB | Monthly Egress (Wasabi)       |
| ------- | --- | ---------------- | --------------- | ----------------------------- |
| 8       | 4K  | 32               | 10 TB           | **Free** (Wasabi) / $100 (B2) |
| 16      | 4K  | 64               | 20 TB           | Free / $200                   |
| 8       | 2K  | 16               | 5 TB            | Free / $50                    |

**Rule:** Local storage = primary. Cloud = backup (events only = 5–10% of local).
