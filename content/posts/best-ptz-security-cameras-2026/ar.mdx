## مقدمة: The Flexibility of PTZ

Pan-Tilt-التكبير/التصغير (PTZ) cameras can be remotely controlled to change their viewing angle – they pan, tilt, and zoom to cover areas that would otherwise require multiple fixed cameras. Ideal for large rooms, properties, or situations where you want to adjust the camera as needed.

## PTZ Buying Guide

**Range of Motion:** 360° pan + 90° tilt is standard. More tilt (114° on the Tapo C210) is better for ceiling mounting.

**التكبير/التصغير النوع:** Optical zoom (lenses physically move) is far superior. Digital zoom is just pixel magnification.

**Motion Tracking:** Automatic tracking of people or vehicles – useful, but can be confusing with fast movement.

**Privacy:** PTZ cameras cannot monitor areas outside their field of view. Plan for multiple cameras or combine with fixed cameras.
