## Solar Camera Buying Guide

**Solar Panel Size:** 6W or larger for reliable charging even on cloudy days. Smaller panels (3-4W) need direct sunlight.

**Battery Capacity:** 6000mAh+ for bridging sun-poor days. Larger batteries are better for northern latitudes.

**Panel Type:** Detachable panels are more flexible to position. Integrated panels are more compact but less adjustable.

**Location:** South-facing (northern hemisphere) for maximum sunlight. At least 4 hours of direct sun per day recommended.
