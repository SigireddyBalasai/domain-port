## Introdução

Indoor security cameras should put your mind at ease – not give you more worries. Unlike outdoor cameras that must withstand wind and weather, indoor cameras need to be one thing above all: discreet, reliable, and privacy-friendly.

Most people need an indoor camera to monitor pets, children, elderly relatives, or simply to deter burglars. But many also have concerns: Will the camera be hacked? Who has access to my recordings? What happens to my data if I cancel the subscription?

We tested over 30 indoor cameras based on these criteria: image quality, motion detection, app reliability, privacy features, storage options, and value for money.

## Privacy for Indoor Cameras

Indoor cameras have access to your private life. Privacy should be a priority.

**Physical Privacy Switch:** Cameras like the Wyze Cam v4 have a physical switch that disconnects camera and microphone.

**Privacy Zones:** Select areas in the image that should not be recorded (e.g., a window).

**Local Armazenamento:** Store footage on microSD instead of the cloud.

**On-Device AI:** AI processing on the camera instead of in the cloud.

**Two-Factor Authentication:** Always enable.

**Network Segmentation:** Place cameras on a separate Wi-Fi network or VLAN.
