## Important Features for Child Monitoring

**Low Latency:** Delay under 1 second for real-time communication.

**Two-Way Audio:** Comfort or call the child without having to enter the room.

**Night Vision:** Good IR night vision for the nursery at night.

**Privacy:** Physical switch or privacy mode when the camera is not needed.

**Temperature and Humidity Sensor:** Some cameras have built-in sensors for room climate.

**Motion and Sound Detection:** Notification on crying or unusual sounds.

## Our Verdict

The **Wyze Cam v4** is the best camera for kids' rooms – affordable, 2K resolution, two-way audio, and privacy mode. For PTZ functions we recommend the **TP-Link Tapo C210**, for maximum privacy the **Eufy Indoor Cam 2K**.
