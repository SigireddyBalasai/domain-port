## Introduction: Safety for Seniors

Security cameras for elderly people need to be one thing above all: easy to use. Complex apps, confusing settings, and complicated installation are often a barrier for seniors.

We tested cameras that stand out with voice control, large buttons in the app, two-way audio (speaking without a phone), and simple setup.

## Important Features for Seniors

**Voice Control:** Display camera feed on a smart display via voice command (Echo Show, Google Nest Hub).

**Two-Way Audio:** Speak with the person in front of the camera – without needing a phone.

**Simple App:** Large buttons, clear labels, not cluttered.

**Automatic Notifications:** Push notifications on motion – no constant checking required.

**Privacy:** Physical privacy switch or privacy mode for peace of mind.

## Our Verdict

The **Wyze Cam v4** is the best camera for seniors – affordable, simple app, voice control, and privacy mode. For Alexa users we recommend the **Ring Stick Up Cam**, for Google users the **Google Nest Cam Indoor**.
