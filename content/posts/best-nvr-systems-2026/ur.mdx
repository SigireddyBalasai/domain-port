## NVR Buying Guide

**چینلز:** Plan the number of cameras plus 2-4 reserve for expansion. An 8-channel NVR is sufficient for most homes.

**ذخیرہ:** 2TB stores about 80-130 days of motion-triggered footage at 4K (4 cameras). 4TB+ for 24/7 recording.

**PoE Ports:** Built-in PoE ports simplify installation. Plan for one port per camera.

**ریزولیوشن:** Make sure the NVR supports your camera resolution (4K-capable).

**Compatibility:** ONVIF compliance allows using different camera brands.

**H.265/H.265+:** Saves storage space without quality loss. Reolink's H.265+ saves up to 50% compared to H.265.
