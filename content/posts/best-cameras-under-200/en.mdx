This guide covers the best CCTV cameras under $200, focusing on PoE models for reliability and WiFi options for flexible installation. Each pick is evaluated on video quality, night performance, build durability, and value for money.

## What should you look for in a camera under $200?

- **Resolution**: 4K UltraHD at $130 (Amcrest), 4MP at $60 (Reolink), or 2K at $80 (TP-Link)
- **Connection**: PoE (single cable for power + data) is preferred, but WiFi works when cabling isn't feasible
- **Night Vision**: Look for Starlight sensors at this price point — they outperform standard IR in low light
- **Smart Detection**: Person/vehicle detection is now standard at $60+
- **Weatherproof Rating**: IP66 minimum for outdoor installation
