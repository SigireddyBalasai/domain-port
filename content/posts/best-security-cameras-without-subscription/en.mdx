## Introduction: No Subscription, No Problem

Monthly fees for security cameras can add up quickly. At $10/month per camera, you pay over $1,200 over 10 years – per camera. Fortunately, there are excellent cameras that work completely without a subscription.

These cameras store locally on microSD cards, NVR hard drives, or NAS systems. You pay once and own the camera forever.

## Subscription-Free vs. Cloud: The True Costs

**Subscription-Free (Local):**

- One-time costs: Camera + storage card
- Ongoing costs: $0
- 10-year cost: Camera + $20-30 SD card

**Cloud Subscription ($10/month):**

- One-time costs: Camera
- Ongoing costs: $120/year
- 10-year cost: Camera + $1,200

## Our Verdict

The **Wyze Cam v4** is the best subscription-free camera for indoor use. For outdoor areas we recommend the **Reolink Argus 3 Pro**. If you need 4K, go with the **Amcrest IP8M-2496B** or **Reolink RLC-822A**.
