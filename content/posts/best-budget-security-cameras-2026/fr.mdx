## Analyses détaillées

### 1. Wyze Cam v4 – Best Overall Under $40

**Prix :** $35.99 | **Résolution :** 2K | **Stockage :** microSD up to 256 GB

The Wyze Cam v4 offers 2K resolution, AI detection, two-way audio, and local storage for under $40. The physical privacy switch completely disables the camera and microphone.

**Avantages :** 2K, AI detection, physical privacy mode, microSD, Alexa/Google.

**Inconvénients :** 2.4GHz Wi-Fi only, occasional false alerts.

**Notre verdict:** The best budget indoor camera on the market.

## Budget Camera Guidelines

- **Under $30:** Expect 1080p and cloud storage. Good for indoor use.
- **$30-50:** 2K and local storage possible. The sweet spot for indoor cameras.
- **$50-80:** Here you'll find PTZ features or better outdoor options.
- **$80-100:** Solid outdoor cameras with battery/solar or PoE.

## Notre verdict

For most people, the **Wyze Cam v4** is the best budget camera. It offers 2K, AI detection, and local storage for $35.99. If you need an outdoor camera, go with the **TP-Link Tapo C500** or **Zmodo Outdoor Pro**.
