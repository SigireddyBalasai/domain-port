## Análises detalhadas

### 1. Wyze Cam v4 – Best Overall Under $40

**Preço:** $35.99 | **Resolução:** 2K | **Armazenamento:** microSD up to 256 GB

The Wyze Cam v4 offers 2K resolution, AI detection, two-way audio, and local storage for under $40. The physical privacy switch completely disables the camera and microphone.

**Prós:** 2K, AI detection, physical privacy mode, microSD, Alexa/Google.

**Contras:** 2.4GHz Wi-Fi only, occasional false alerts.

**Nosso veredito:** The best budget indoor camera on the market.

---

### 2. TP-Link Tapo C210 – Best PTZ Camera Under $50

**Preço:** $44.99 | **Resolução:** 2K | **Armazenamento:** microSD up to 512 GB

360° pan and 114° tilt for under $50. Ideal for monitoring large rooms.

**Prós:** PTZ, 2K, microSD up to 512 GB, Alexa/Google.

**Contras:** Motion detection only in current field of view, PTZ mechanism is audible.

---

### 3. Kasa Spot KL125P2 – Cheapest Camera

**Preço:** $24.99 | **Resolução:** 1080p | **Armazenamento:** microSD up to 512 GB

The cheapest camera on the list. Two cameras for under $50.

**Prós:** Extremely affordable, microSD up to 512 GB, Alexa/Google.

**Contras:** 1080p only, no continuous recording.

**Prós:** Very compact, 143°, Alexa.

**Prós:** 2K, on-device AI, no subscription, local storage.

## Budget Camera Guidelines

- **Under $30:** Expect 1080p and cloud storage. Good for indoor use.
- **$30-50:** 2K and local storage possible. The sweet spot for indoor cameras.
- **$50-80:** Here you'll find PTZ features or better outdoor options.
- **$80-100:** Solid outdoor cameras with battery/solar or PoE.

## Nosso veredito

For most people, the **Wyze Cam v4** is the best budget camera. It offers 2K, AI detection, and local storage for $35.99. If you need an outdoor camera, go with the **TP-Link Tapo C500** or **Zmodo Outdoor Pro**.
