---
question: "Do I need permission to install CCTV cameras?"
category: "legal"
order: 4
tags: ["legal", "permission", "regulations"]
---

In most areas, you can install CCTV on your property **without permission**. However, there are important considerations:

- Cameras should **not record public spaces** or neighbors' properties without consent
- Check **local regulations** and HOA rules before installation
- Some areas require **signage** notifying people of surveillance
- Audio recording may have **additional legal restrictions**

Always consult local laws before installing surveillance equipment.
