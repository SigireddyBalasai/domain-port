---
question: "Can CCTV cameras work in the dark?"
category: "features"
order: 6
tags: ["night-vision", "infrared", "features"]
---

Yes, most modern CCTV cameras have **infrared (IR) LEDs** for night vision, allowing them to capture clear footage in complete darkness up to **30-100 feet**.

Night vision technologies include:

- **IR LEDs**: Standard night vision, black and white footage
- **Color night vision**: Uses built-in spotlights for color footage
- **Starlight sensors**: Enhanced low-light color capture
- **Thermal imaging**: Detects heat signatures, works in total darkness

For best results, ensure cameras are positioned to avoid IR reflection from nearby surfaces.
