Storage duration depends on your needs:

- **Homes**: 7-14 days is usually sufficient
- **Small businesses**: 30 days recommended
- **Large businesses**: 30-90 days

Factors affecting storage needs:

| Factor            | Impact                         |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Number of cameras | More cameras = more storage    |
| Resolution        | Higher resolution = more space |
| Motion detection  | Reduces storage needs          |
| Local regulations | May require minimum retention  |
