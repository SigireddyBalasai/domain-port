---
question: "What is the difference between IP and analog CCTV cameras?"
category: "basics"
order: 3
tags: ["ip-camera", "analog", "comparison"]
---

**IP (Internet Protocol) cameras** transmit digital data over a network, offering:

- Higher resolution (up to 4K and beyond)
- Remote access from anywhere
- Advanced features like motion detection and AI analytics
- Power over Ethernet (PoE) capability

**Analog cameras** use coaxial cables and DVRs, typically offering:

- Lower resolution (usually 1080p max)
- Lower cost per camera
- Simpler setup for small systems
- Reliable performance with less network dependency
